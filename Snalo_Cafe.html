About Us

Kaya Café was founded by Snalo Mtyhi on April 10, 2026 — a day that marks both a personal milestone and the beginning of a dream.

Inspired by the warmth of coffee culture and the beauty of human connection, Kaya Café was created as a space where people can slow down, share moments, and feel a sense of belonging.

Our vision is to grow beyond borders — bringing people together through coffee and small bites, and building a community that connects locally and internationally.

Welcome to Kaya Café – a place of warmth and connection.
